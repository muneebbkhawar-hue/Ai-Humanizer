How MetaWorld Humanizer Works

1. PASTE YOUR TEXT
Paste any AI-generated content. The tool splits it into individual sentences and processes each one separately.
2. CHOOSE A STYLE & STRENGTH
Natural – everyday conversational tone. Academic – formal but varied. Casual – relaxed, breezy. Formal – professional prose.

Strength controls how aggressively the sentence structure is changed.
3. PICK YOUR VARIANTS
Each sentence gets 5 rewrites. Click any variant to select it. The assembled result updates live at the bottom.
4. COPY OR DOWNLOAD
Copy the assembled output to clipboard or download as a .txt file.
✦ PRO TIPS FOR BEST RESULTS
  • Use Heavy strength for text flagged by multiple detectors
  • Mix variants — don't just pick all A's or all B's
  • Re-run the output through the tool a second time for extra safety
  • For academic text, use Academic style to keep the register
  • Short sentences (< 20 words) are harder for detectors to flag
